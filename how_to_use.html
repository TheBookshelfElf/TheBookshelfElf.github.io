The Bookshelf Elf

Step 1

Choose whether you want to search for a book similar to another book or for a book with certain features by clicking an option on the home page!

Step 2

If you want to search by features, check all the options you want in your book to have so they show a check mark and then click "Start Search"!

If you want to search for a similar book, type in the author, name, or keyword of the book. Scroll through the results until you find the one you are looking for and then click the select button for that book!

Step 3

Scroll through the results until you find a book that interests you! When you do, click "read more" to learn more about it! The books are organized by how close they are to what you are looking for.
